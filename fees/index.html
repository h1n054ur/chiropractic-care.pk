Chiropractic Health and Wellness Clinic strives to make quality chiropractic services accessible to the masses in several ways – without sacrificing quality of care. They are:

  • Ensuring operational and overhead costs are as low as possible. Our chiropractic clinic is deliberately kept small so we can offer personalised treatment to each of our patients. This also minimises our overheads and operational costs – a key factor in how we keep our chiropractor fees affordable.
  • Reducing treatment time. We achieve this by always applying the most effective, individualised treatment plan for fast pain relief and a speedy recovery. We’ll also reduce the overall number of sessions you’ll need by introducing chiropractic maintenance care during an earlier stage of your therapy.
  • Telling you if chiropractic care is not suitable in your case. If we believe we can’t help you, we’ll immediately refer you to another form of healthcare provider. This will save you both time and money.
  • No hidden extras. If it turns out that an extra treatment method is necessary during your chiropractic session, there’ll be no additional charge in most cases.
  • No pressure. We’ll never advise a patient to agree to a multi-visit package unless it’ll save you money.
  • Free advice. As a value-added service, we’ll advise you on lifestyle matters such as exercise and nutrition to help you stay healthy in the future.
  • No unnecessary X-rays. Most chiropractic patients don’t need X-rays, but some chiropractors take them as routine. Dr Salman Khan can determine whether a bone is broken and needs an X-ray. This also benefits the patient by no unnecessary exposure to radiation.
