Back pain treatment

Back pain is a leading cause of disability, and the single most common cause of lower back pain is a pulled or torn ligament and/or muscle.

Our upper and lower back pain treatment typically entails spinal manipulation to fix strained muscles and ligaments, herniated discs, and muscle spasms.

CTS & other hand and wrist pain treatment

We provide treatment of carpal tunnel syndrome (CTS) and other hand and wrist pains.

CTS occurs when the median nerve going into the hand from the neck becomes compressed. This and other wrist problems can be treated with:

  • Trigger point therapy – manual compression and stretching of tight areas of muscle tissue.
  • Manipulation of the muscles to improve flexibility and blood circulation.

Elbow pain treatment

Sciatica & other nerve pain treatment

Without effective treatment, nerve problems like sciatica and a pinched nerve can lead to serious complications.

Our treatment of sciatica and other nerve pains fixes misalignment of hard and soft tissue to ease pressure on the nerve, and often involves correcting a herniated disc or muscle strain.

Spinal traction – painless spinal decompression therapy – is a particularly effective treatment for sciatica.

Scoliosis treatment

Our scoliosis treatment for back pain addresses the problem of irregular sideways curvature of the spine.

Dr Liew does this by using chiropractic techniques such as:

  • Activator method – applying a small spring-loaded instrument to the spine.
  • Gentle spinal traction – to relax muscles and move the spine into a more central position.

Shoulder pain treatment

Dr David Liew’s shoulder pain treatment entails chiropractic adjustments to realign the joint and surrounding tissues. These procedures may involve:

  • The drop-piece technique – assisting the chiropractic thrust while minimising the force needed.
  • Trigger point therapy – finger-tip pressure to ease pain in muscles.
  • Manual manipulation – applied to joints and muscle tissue to relieve ligament stiffness.

Slipped disc treatment

Like scoliosis chiropractic therapy, our slipped disc treatment can help to prevent serious complications as well as relieve discomfort.

A slipped disc in the back can be treated by flexing the spine and stretching muscles to expand spaces between vertebrae.
