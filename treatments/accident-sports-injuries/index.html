Chiropractic treatment after an accident

Some typical injuries sustained in a traffic accident, such as broken bones, can be diagnosed relatively quickly. Others may not be immediately apparent. For instance, headaches after a car crash could be a result of whiplash caused by a severe jolt.

Other common problems resulting from traffic accidents include:

  • Back issues such as a slipped disc or other spinal cord damage.
  • Soft tissue damage when muscles, tendons, or ligaments are stretched or torn.

Common injury signs and symptoms after an accident

Symptoms of a spinal column injury include:

  • A dull or sharp pain that may come and go or be constant.
  • Tightness or stiffness in muscles.

Without back injury treatment, the problem can result in more serious issues such as weakness or numbness in the legs or arms.

Symptoms of a ligament sprain or muscle or tendon strain include:

  • Pain
  • Swelling
  • Bruising

Untreated sprains and strains can cause wear and tear on cartilage that can result in problems such as arthritis.

Symptoms of a whiplash injury include:

  • Neck stiffness or pain
  • Tingling sensation
  • Restricted range of motion.
  • Back and shoulder pain
  • Headaches

Without whiplash injury treatment, the problem can result in chronic pain lasting a lifetime.

Benefits of chiropractic treatment after an accident

Benefits of treatment by a back-injury doctor of chiropractic after an auto accident include:

  • Non-invasive, natural, drug-free treatment.
  • Reduction of inflammation and scar tissue.
  • Avoiding long-term pain.
  • Restoring the body’s mobility.

Chiropractic techniques used by Dr David Liew to treat soft tissue damage, such as muscle strains, after a car accident include myofascial release and the active release technique.

Myofascial release relaxes contracted muscles and improves blood circulation in the myofascial connective tissue that support muscles.

The active release technique eases tight muscles and nerve trigger points to alleviate joint stress and muscular pains.

Another common spinal injury treatment after a traffic collision is spinal traction – gentle pressure to mobilise ligaments and muscles to create more space between the bones in your back.

Our lower back injury treatments following an accident include the direct thrust technique, in which a hand push is applied to correct a misaligned vertebra to improve functionality and restore mobility.

Our chiropractic neck injury treatments include cervical distraction, which entails easing compression of nerves in the neck.

Chiropractic treatment after a sports injury

Common injuries that sports enthusiasts suffer include:

  • Ankle sprain.
  • Groin strain.
  • Hamstring strain – in the tendons or muscles in the back of the thigh.
  • Knee ligament damage.
  • Tennis elbow (epicondylitis).

Symptoms of sprains and strains like these include:

  • Restricted mobility of joints.
  • A popping sound or sensation at the time of the injury.

Ignoring the symptoms of soft tissue damage can result in chronic conditions like arthritis.

Benefits of chiropractic care after a sports injury

Besides pain relief, benefits of chiropractic care after a sports injury include restoration of:

  • Range of motion

In cases of soft tissue damage, Dr David Liew can relieve pain and restore mobility with the help of shockwave therapy. It involves a series of high-energy impulses to encourage healing by producing an anti-inflammatory response in the body.

In instances of a herniated disc, the activator method can be effective. This entails use of a hand-held, spring-loaded device called an activator adjusting instrument to apply pressure to the spine with a low-force impulse.
