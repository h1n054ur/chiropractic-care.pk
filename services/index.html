Chiropractic treatment for scoliosis and infant torticollis

Chiropractic Health and Wellness Clinic specialises in:

Scoliosis in children

Scoliosis is the irregular curvature of the spine to the side. This can be a congenital problem but most often starts when youngsters are aged 10 to 15. In these cases, the condition can be caused by an infection or muscle spasms.

Signs of scoliosis include protruding ribs and uneven shoulders.

Infant torticollis

Torticollis (twisted neck) can result from a difficult birth or how the baby was positioned in the womb.

Left untreated, infant torticollis (also known as congenital muscular torticollis), can lead to contraction of the spine, misshapen skull bones, and restricted shoulder movement.

Dr David Liew has a remarkable 100% success rate in treatment for infant torticollis.

Prenatal chiropractic

A woman’s body has a lot to cope with during pregnancy, and this can cause health problems.

You may get muscle cramps, generally in the foot or calf, and you might frequently feel faint because of hormonal changes. Another possible issue during pregnancy is varicose (swollen) veins in the legs.

As a baby in the womb becomes heavier, it alters your centre of gravity and your posture adjusts to accommodate this shift. This can cause a misaligned spine or joints, resulting in an increased curve in your back.

The International Chiropractic Paediatric Association (ICPA) recommends that women get chiropractic treatment throughout pregnancy to maintain pelvic balance.

Pregnancy chiropractic techniques

Our prenatal chiropractic care provides an effective remedy for issues during pregnancy because it focuses on the connection between the spine, the central nervous system, and the reproductive system.

Dr David Liew applies chiropractic techniques that entail little if any pressure on the abdomen.

These methods include the activator technique – a gentle chiropractic adjustment using a small hand-held instrument to apply light pressure to the spine with a spring-loaded device that delivers a low-force impulse.

The instrument acts quickly so muscles are more likely to remain relaxed and not tense up and resist treatment.

Another chiropractic technique that’s helped many women during pregnancy is myofascial release, a hands-on treatment that entails gentle pressure on the connective tissue that supports muscles.

Myofascial release can restore mobility and ease pain.

As an experienced prenatal chiropractor, Dr David Liew will also advise you on exercises that are safe during pregnancy and on the importance of proper hydration and nutrition – essential for a healthy pregnancy.

Orthotics for feet

Problems with the complex mechanical structures of the feet are one of the most prevalent medical complaints, affecting young and old alike.

One of the most common foot problems is heel pain from plantar fasciitis. Other issues include flat feet, problems with the metatarsal bones in the foot, and bunions.

Foot orthotics is the medical field concerned with devices inserted into footwear to ensure balance and functionality and cushion the foot against impact.

In addition to orthotics for plantar fasciitis, bunion orthotics, arch orthotics (for flat feet) and metatarsal orthotics, our adult and children’s orthotics include orthotics for heel pain, orthotics for under- and over-pronation, and sport orthotics.
